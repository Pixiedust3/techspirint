NCHL PEN TESTING CHALLENGE (48 HOURS)

About the Challenge

The NCHL Pen Testing Challenge is a 48-hour ethical hacking and penetration testing program aimed at promoting responsible security research and strengthening Nepal's digital financial infrastructure. The challenge provides a controlled and authorized environment for cybersecurity practitioners to identify, analyze, and report vulnerabilities in NCHL systems while strictly following ethical and legal guidelines.
